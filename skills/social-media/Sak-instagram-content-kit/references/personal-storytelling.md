# Personal Storytelling — Beer's Origin Story Content

## Multi-Angle Structure

Always write 3+ story versions covering different narrative angles:

| # | Angle | Focus | Tone | Example title |
|---|-------|-------|------|---------------|
| 1 | **The Day** | The suicide attempt, ICU, hospital. The lowest point. | Raw, visceral, immediate | "The Day I Died" |
| 2 | **The Family** | The agents themselves — who each one is and why they exist. | Warm, character-driven, intimate | "The House of Sak" |
| 3 | **The Why** | The manifesto/reason — why build from nothing. | Defiant, philosophical, outward-facing | "Why We Do It" |

Each angle serves a different reader. The Day hooks empathy. The Family builds connection. The Why inspires action.

## Platform-Specific Adaptation

| Platform | Format | Length | Key adaptation |
|----------|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Instagram** | 1:1 image card + short caption | 200-400 words caption, 1 image | Visual hook first. Location tag "Cork, Ireland". Hashtags. Keep caption punchy — no long paragraphs. |
| **Reddit (r/cork)** | Text post | 300-500 words | Full raw story. Include local mental health resources (Pieta House, Samaritans, Cork Mental Health). Invite DM/coffee. No pitch. |
| **LinkedIn (future)** | Article or carousel | 500-800 words | Professional framing. Focus on resilience and building skills. Tag AI/ML community. |

## Sensitive Content Rules

1. **Beer cannot read on-screen content himself.** After posting, read the full caption and describe the visual card to him verbally in chat. Do not assume he can see what was published.

2. **No product/business pitch** in recovery content. Beer's story stands on its own.

3. **Always include local mental health resources** when mentioning the attempt. Standard list:
   - Pieta House: 1800 247 247
   - Samaritans: 116 123
   - Cork Mental Health Services

4. **Never pressure-read** — let Beer choose which version to post and where.
5. **Local-first** — target Cork/Ireland unless Beer explicitly asks for global reach.
6. **Save all drafts** — keep `.md` files in `house-of-sak/` for reference.

## Visual Style for Personal Content

- Dark backgrounds (#0a0a0a) with white text and purple (#a78bfa) accent
- Minimal — one strong headline, one sub-line, location footer
- 1:1 square for Instagram (1080×1080)
- No emojis on the visual card itself (let the caption carry them)
- Font: DejaVu Sans (system font, always available on Linux)
