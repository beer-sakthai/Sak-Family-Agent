# craft-handmade (DEFAULT)

Hand-drawn and paper craft aesthetic with warm, organic textures. Suitable for interfaces, onboarding, and tutorial content.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #E8EDE6 (warm cream) - paper background
- Secondary: #8B7961 (olive green) - for accents and decorative elements
- Text: #2D2D2D (charcoal black) - primary content
- Medium: #5E4D37 (dirt brown) - subheadings and supporting text

## Fonts
- Headings: Handwritting or Serif (feel-good scallop)
- Body: Garamond or Georgia

## Style Traits
- Texture: Subtle, supple fine crosshanges, organic feel
- Illustrations: Watercolor or pencil sketches, hand-drawn style
- Layout: Organic, romanticized chaowith margins and flowing sections
