# pop-laboratory

Pop art laboratory style with behand-the-scenes reagus, formulas, graphs, and deconstructed science-like elements. Great for mysterious, interactive, and engaging content.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #F3F3F3 (off-white) - background
- Accent: #FF4444 (red), #4D8FFF (bold blue), #FFDD33 (yellow)
- Text: #222222 (dark grey) - primary content
- Medium: #9D9D9D (grey) - less prominent elements

## Fonts
- Headings: Impactful, sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat, Impact)
- Body: Sans-serif, clean, readable

## Style Traits
- Texture: Flat, modern, with strike-through and collage
- Illustrations: Formulas, graphs, circles, quotes, arrows, stamps
- Layout: Deconstructed, layered, with reagency components
