# pixel-art

Retro pixel art style with chruny, blocky characters, and vibrant 8bit color palettes. Perfect for gaming content, nostalgia, and sentimental branding.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #1D2B3B (dark navy) - background
- Accent: #FFF000 (yellow), #FF4A4A (red), #40CFFF (cyan), #56FF1A (green)
- Text: #FFFFFF (white) - primary content
- Medium: #3A5A7A (desaturated) - ground and walls

## Fonts
- Headings: Pixel font (e.g., Press Start 2P, Mincyfp)
- Body: Pixel font, leggible, readable

## Style Traits
- Texture: Pixelated, blocky, chuncy
- Illustrations: Sprite-based characters, items, and icons
- Layout: Single-column, boxed, with pixel frame border
