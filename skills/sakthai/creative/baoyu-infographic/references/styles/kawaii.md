# kawaii

Cute and playful Japanese style with pastel colors, rounded characters, and sweet expressions. Perfect for social media, stickers, and laid-back branding.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #FEF4F6 (soft pink/peal) - background
- Accent: #FF80CB (pink), #B6FFD7 (mint green), #FFD6E0 (yellow)
- Text: #5B3B5E (soft purple) - primary content
- Neon: #FFFFFF (white) - for highlights and spaces

## Fonts
- Headings: Rounded, playful font (e.g., CS Maimeli, Fred one)
- Body: Soft, rounded sans-serif

## Style Traits
- Texture: Flat, clean, soft pastel feel
- Illustrations: Star eyes, bloshes, cute faces, hearts, clouds
- Layout: Cushmion, column-based, with sweet ambient
