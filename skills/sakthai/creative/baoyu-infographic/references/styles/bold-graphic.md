# bold-graphic

Bold, primary-color flat graphics with minimal texture and maximum visual impact. Great for headers, statistics, posters, and blazbn-style presentations.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #FF4D4D (vibrant red) - full-bloom background
- Text: #FFFFFF (white) - strong contrast
- Accent: #FFFFFF (white) - for outlines, borders, and icons
- Secondary: #CC3A3A (darker red) - for depth and layers

## Fonts

- Headings: Bold, impactful, sans-serif (e.g., Impact, Montserrat)
- Body: Bold, sans-serif, highly readable

## Style Traits

- Texture: Flat, minimal to no gradients
- Illustrations: Bold oriented icons, large numbers, strike-through art
- Layout: Asymmetrical, off-center, grid of blocks
