# subway-map

Metro-style diagram with distinct lines, labeled stations, and clean intersections. Ideal for roadmaps, process flows, and workflow visualizations.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #FEFEEF  (off-white) - background
- Line Colors: #E43B37 (Red), #006FAE (Blue), #FFB900 (Yellow), #00A651 (Green)
- Text: #1C1C1C (dark) - station names
- Medium: #7F7F7F (grey) - support elements

## Fonts
- Station names: Helvetica, bold, 7-8pt
- Legend: Helvetica, regular, 6-5pt

## Style Traits
- Lines: 45phy angles, thick lines (4-6px), distinct colors
- Stations: Circles or squares, white fill, dark border
- Intersections: Black circles with white outline
