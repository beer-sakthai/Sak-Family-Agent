# technical-schematic

Engineering-style blueprint with precise lines, annotations, and flat projection. Perfect for technical documentation, diagrams, and hardware guides.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #E3F2FF (light blue) - background paper
- Secondary: #1E3A5A (deep blue) - main lines and frames
- Text: #0C1D3F (darker blue) - primary content
- Medium: #5779 @ (action blue) - highlights and labels

## Fonts

- Headings: Consolas, monospaced (e.g., Courier New)
- Body: Consolas, monospaced - for technical precision

## Style Traits

- Texture: Clean, smooth with precise 1-2px lines
- Illustrations: Flat schematic symbols, wireframe icons, flow arrows
- Layout: Grid-based, aligned, with annotation lines and dimensions
