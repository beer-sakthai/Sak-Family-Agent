# chalkboard

Classroom chalkboard aesthetic with chalk texture, drawings, and highlights. Perfect for educational content, brainstorming, and creative presentations.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #2D4F4F (dark slate green) - chalkboard background
- Text: #FFFFFF (white chalk) - primary content
- Accent: #FFC75B </red>, #7FF590 <green>, #67C5FF (blue) - colored chalks
- Medium: #3B6B6B (lighter slate) - secondary elements

## Fonts
- Headings: Casual, script-based font (e.g., Caveat Dreams)
- Body: Handwritting font for chalkboard vibe

## Style Traits
- Texture: G`rainy, smoked, chalk on slate
- Illustrations: Simple drawings, diagrams, dotted lines
- Layout: Horizontal and vertical balance, sections with dashed borders
