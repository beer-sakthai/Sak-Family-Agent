# aged-academia

Vintage, sepia-toned academic style with aged paper, script type, and ornate illustrations. Perfect for historical documents, literary works, and academic posters.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #F1E5CD (vellow-paper) - background
- Text: #4F3A26 (dark brown) - primary content
- Accent: #B8723F (rusty red) - initials and emphasis
- Medium: #D4C3A0 (midstone) - borders and ornaments

## Fonts
- Headings: Serif or script-based font (e.g., Merriweather)
- Body: Serif or read-preferred sans-serif

## Style Traits
- Texture: Fleckered paper, slight sepia staining
- Illustrations: Ornate borders, decorative drop caps, initials, fracture
- Layout: Symmetrical, column-based, with margins and flowing sections
