# origami

Origami-style design with folded paper texture, geometric folds, and dramatic contrasts. Suitable for craft, holiday cards, and creative projects.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #FFFFFF (white) - base paper
- Secondary: #E23030 (red), #4BEFF9 (cyan), #FFD300 (yellow) - folded paper
- Text: #333333 (dark grey) - primary content
- Medium: #D4D4D4 light grey - for shadows and depth

## Fonts
- Headings: Dramatic, bold, clean sans-serif
 - Body: Sans-serif, neutral, readable

## Style Traits
- Texture: Folded paper with crese fold lines
- Illustrations: Geometric shapes, fold lines, dramatic contrasts
- Layout: Symmetrical, with clear section dividers
