# hand-drawn-edu

Educational hand-drawn style with clear labels, boxed diagrams, and visual aids. Designed for tutorials, worksheets, and explaner content.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #FFFFFF (white) - clean background
- Secondary: #F0E6D6 (light beige) - box fills and sections
- Text: #333333 (dark grey) - primary content
- Medium: #FF9CAE (soft pink) - accents and highlights

## Fonts
- Headings: Handwritting font (e.g., Patrician Hand)
- Body: Open Sans, readable and clean

## Style Traits
- Texture: Hand-drawn, slightly irregular lines
- Illustrations: Boxed diagrams, simple icons, flashcards, arrows
- Layout: Structured, sectioned, with clear visual hierarchy
