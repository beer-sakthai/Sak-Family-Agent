# knolling

Systematic and knolling style with parallel-arranged objects, clean background, and labeled display. Perfect for catalogs, inventories, and structured overviews.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #FEFEEF <off-white> - background
- Objects without outline: flat color blocks in row and column
- Text: #333333 (dark grey) - primary content
- Accent: #0077BB (deep blue) - highlight names and labels

## Fonts

- Headings: Bold sans-serif (e.g., Fira Code)
- Body: Sans-serif, clean, neutral

## Style Traits

- Texture: Flat, clean, with soft shadows for depth
- Illustrations: Parallel-arranged objects in a step-by-step manner
- Layout: Grid-based, rows and columns, with labels underneath
