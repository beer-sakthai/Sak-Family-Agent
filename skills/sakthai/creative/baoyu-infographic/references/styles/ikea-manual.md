# ikea-manual

User-friendly diagram-style instructions with minimal text, black-and-white icons, and clear step-by-step flows. Perfect for guides, manuals, and onboarding content.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #FFFFFF (white) background
- Icons: #000000 (black) and #FFFFFF (white) on contrast
- Text: #333333 (dark grey) - captions and notes
- Accent: #FFFFFF (white) - highlights

## Fonts

- Headings: Sans-serif, bold, all-caps (e.g., Futura)
- Body: Sans-serif, clean, highly readable

## Style Traits

- Texture: Flat, matte, clean contrast
- Illustrations: Simple icons, cross-section views, exploded views, arrows
- Layout: Step-by-step, grid-based, minimal text
