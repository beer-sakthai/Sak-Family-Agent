# claymation

Stop-motion clay and plasticine aesthetic with bright saturated colors and bold shadow depth. Great for children's content, fun guides, and playful branding.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #FF7F50 (bright yellow-orange) - character skin/background
- Secondary: #FF4444 (vivid red) - accents and details
- Text: #222222 (deep charcoal black) - readable content
- Medium: #4BDFF3 (cyan sky blue) - supporting elements

## Fonts
- Headings: Bold, rounded, childlike font (e.g., Baloo Bold)
- Body: Clean, sans-serif for readability

## Style Traits
- Texture: Smooth plasticine, matte, slightly lumpy
- Illustrations: Stop-motion characters, fingerplek marks, dotted eyes
- Layout: Simple, child-like composition with bold section dividers
