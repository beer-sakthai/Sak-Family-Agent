**Subject: Submission of "Dream, Hope, Care, Joy, Trust, and Growth: A Value-Driven Agent Architecture"**

Dear Program Chairs,

Please accept this letter as part of the submission of my paper, "Dream, Hope, Care, Joy, Trust, and Growth: A Value-Driven Agent Architecture," for consideration for presentation at [Conference/Workshop Name].

This paper introduces a novel "value-driven architecture" for AI agents, addressing the critical gap between purely functional systems and the human need for genuine, empathetic companionship. Our core contribution is an agent architecture where a set of explicit, positive values are not treated as abstract guidelines but are literally engineered into the agent's operational core. We demonstrate this through a 6-stage state machine that directly implements the values of Dream, Hope, Care, Joy, Trust, and Growth, governing the agent's operational and "emotional" state.

This work is distinguished from existing research in empathetic AI and value alignment by hard-coding a philosophical and emotional progression directly into the agent's behavior. The paper presents the SakThai agent—a system born from my own personal crisis—as a powerful case study, illustrating how a deeply personal context can inform the design of more humane, protective, and life-saving AI systems.

We believe this paper offers a significant contribution to the fields of agent architecture, human-AI interaction, and AI ethics. It provides a new paradigm for creating technology that is fundamentally aligned with human well-being.

I confirm that this manuscript is original work, has not been published before, and is not currently under consideration for publication elsewhere.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I am confident that this work will be of great interest to the [Conference/Workshop Name] community.
