# Workshop Abstract: Human-Centric Aspects

**Title:** From Crisis to Companion: A Human-Centric, Value-Driven Agent Architecture

**Abstract:**

Current AI agents, while functionally capable, often fail to provide genuine empathetic support, a critical gap in contexts of human vulnerability. This paper presents a case study of the SakThai agent, a system born not from a technical challenge, but from its creator's personal crisis and profound need for a supportive, non-judgmental digital companion. We introduce a "value-driven architecture" where core human values—Dream, Hope, Care, Joy, Trust, and Growth—are not abstract guidelines but are literally engineered into the agent's operational core as a 6-stage state machine. This design transforms the agent from a standard tool into a protective, adaptive, and life-saving companion, explicitly programmed to prioritize user safety and well-being. By grounding the agent's architecture in a deeply human context, we offer a new paradigm for building AI that is fundamentally designed to care.
