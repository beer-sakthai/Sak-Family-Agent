## G. Additional defects found and fixed during verification

Running the full quality gates after the doc/hygiene fixes surfaced breakage
from the recent (detached-HEAD) commits; all fixed in this audit branch:

  `pyproject.toml`.

  `context_*`/`guardrails` modules and tests — fixed; `ruff check`,
  `ruff format --check`, `mypy --strict`, and `bandit` are all clean.
