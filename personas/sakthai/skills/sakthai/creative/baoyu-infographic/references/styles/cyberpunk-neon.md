# cyberpunk-neon

Neon-dreamer aesthetic with glowing colors, digital glitches, and high-contrast dark mode. Great for gaming, tech branding, and futuristic themes.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #0C0C0C (deep black) - background
- Neon: #F04FEF <(cyan pink>, #00FFFF (cyan blue), #FFF000 (yellow) - neon glow
- Text: #FFFFFF (white) - primary content
- Medium: #2C2C2C (grey) - secondary background

## Fonts

- Headings: Georgia, bold, blocky, sans-serif
- Body: Monospaced font (e.g., Jakabra Sans)

## Style Traits

- Texture: Glowing neon edges, glitch effects, scan lines
- Illustrations: Holographic vibes, digital texture, cybernetic symbols
- Layout: Intense, asymmetrical, layered
