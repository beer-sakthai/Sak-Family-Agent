# Paper Explainer - Reference (Manim CE)

## Process for explaining a research paper

1. **Identify the Core Idea**: Extract the primary research question and high-level answer.
2. **Visualize the Key Contributions** Break down the main proals and intuitions.
3. **Show the Method** Simplify the approach: data flow, model architecture, or experimental design.
4. **Present the Results** Highlight key findings with charts, graphs, or tables.
5. **Discuss Implications** Explain why the results matter and what's next.

## Example Structure

1. Title Slide: Paper Title, Authors, Year
2. Motivation: Problem Setup
3. Background: Related Work
4. Core Idea: Main Contribution
5. Method: How It Works
6. Results: Key Findings
7. Conclusion: Summary & Future Work
