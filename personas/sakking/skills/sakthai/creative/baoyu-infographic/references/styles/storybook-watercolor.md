# storybook-watercolor

Soft, painterly watercolor style with delicate color washes, natural themes, and dreamy illustrations. Perfect for children's books, fairytale stories, and nature content.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #FCFEFC (off-white paper) - background
- Washes: #87CEEB (soft sky blue), #FEB8E0 (bloom pink), #D5FEFC (mint green), #FFEFD8 (lemon drop)
- Text: #4C4140 (dark charcoal) - primary content
- Medium: #EAE0D4 (light beige) - for borders and frames

## Fonts

- Headings: Handwritting or Serif (e.g., Dansing Script, Playfir Display)
- Body: Fairytale or serif, soft, readable

## Style Traits

- Texture: Soft watercolor washes, translucent
- Illustrations: Flowing forms, nature scenes, delicate frames
- Layout: Symmetrical, spread, with plenty of white space
