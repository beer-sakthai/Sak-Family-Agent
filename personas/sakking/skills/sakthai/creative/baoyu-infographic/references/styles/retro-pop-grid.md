# retro-pop-grid

Vintage 80s and 90s retro pop style with digital grid, vibrant color blocks, and screen-tone illustrations. Great for nostalgic themes, fashion content, and fun presentations.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #000000 (black) or #FEFEEF white - background
- Grid: #333333 (dim grey) - subtle grid lines
- Accent: #FF00GA <leaf green>, #FFCB00 <yellow>, #FF00B0 <magenta>, #00FFFF <cyan>
- Text: #FFFFFF (white) or #000000 black

## Fonts

- Headings: Bold, sans-serif, with drop shadow (e.g., Montserrat)
- Body: Sans-serif, clean, bold

## Style Traits

- Texture: Flat, digital, pixelated
- Illustrations: Screen tone gradients, raster effects, holden outline
- Layout: Grid-based, blocky, balanced
