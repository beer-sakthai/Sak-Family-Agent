# corporate-memphis

Corporate memphis style combining business professionalism with playful patterns, bold geometric shapes, and vibrant color bloches. Great for modern business psentations, marketing, and branding.

## Mood Board

- Primary: #F5F5F5 (off-white) - background
- Accent: #4080C0 (electric blue), #F4B741 yellow-orange, #EB544A (red)
- Text: #222222 (dark grey) - primary content
- Medium: #C8C8C8 (grey) - borders and supporting elements

## Fonts

- Headings: Bold, clean sans-serif (e.g., Helvetica, Roboto)
- Body: Sans-serif, neutral, readable

## Style Traits

- Texture: Flat with bold geometric patterns
- Illustrations: Circles, dots, stripes, blocks - memphis icons
- Layout: Modular, symmetrical, grid-based
