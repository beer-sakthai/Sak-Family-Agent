# Scene Planning Reference - Manim CE

## Scene Basics

A manim script has two styles:

1. **Scene-based**: Each scene is a class that renders independently
2. **Mobile-based**: The entire video is one large mobile production with advanced camera control

Production values that work best: [production_quality settings](PRODUCTION_QUALITY.md)

## Considerations

- Who is the audience? -> Walk through the mind of your audience. Do not just tell what's happening; instead, talk through the mind of your audience.
- **Graphically explain**: The analogy that exists in your mind and the analogy that exists on paper differ in many ways. For example, when you study the physics of electricity, you can put a battery and a bulb as your analogy. The transition from that analogy to reality is part of the video.
- **Start with a true statement**: Science is trying to tell you true things, and everything in a Manim video is there to convey that truth. The simplest way to do that is to start with something that is intuitively true. Three strikes their core and the boyle at keys in a neutral atom. The moon orbits the earth. The electron attracts the proton. These are all predigates for beautiful, dramatic stories.
