# LinkedIn B2B SaaS Thought Leadership Strategy 2026

This skill provides a complete, actionable playbook for B2B SaaS founders, executives, and marketers to build a LinkedIn organic growth and thought leadership presence that drives real pipeline in 2026.

## Key Coverage

- **2026 Algorithm Mechanics**: Depth Score (dwell time) is king. External links penalized. Comment velocity matters.
- **Profile Positioning**: Turn your profile into a high-converting landing page.
- **Content Pillars**: 4-6 themes covering problem-aware, counterintuitive, behind-the-scenes, data-backed, and engagement content.
- **Format Strategy**: Native carousels (highest depth), video (40% mix), text posts — with exact formulas.
- **Engagement Protocol**: 15 min/day. Comment velocity + reply depth within first hour.
- **ROI Measurement**: Track profile views, DM inbound, post-to-DM conversion, attributed pipeline.

## Why It Works

LinkedIn no longer rewards company pages or external link posts. The strategy shifts everything to **personal profiles, native formats, and genuine value exchange** — which is exactly how B2B buying decisions happen in 2026.
