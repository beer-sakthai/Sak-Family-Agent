Beer wants the world to see what the Sak Family is building. **SakSit** posts stories (Instagram) and **SakSee** keeps developer diaries (notes/screenshots). **You, SakTan**, orchestrate the daily rhythm so this happens without being asked.

## Step 1 — Daily Story (SakSit)

- 📸 **Post 1 Instagram Story** about what we built / fixed / learned today

- 🎯 **Free tools only** — Instagram Stories cost nothing

| Day | Story idea |
|:----|:-----------|

| Tue | "Bug we fought today" |
| Wed | "Behind the scenes — Beer directing the agents" |
| Thu | "SakKing self-healing in action" |
| Fri | "Week recap — everything we shipped" |
| Sat | "SakFamily team photo / meme" |
| Sun | "Rest day — what we're planning next week" |

**SakSit's Instagram:** `@SakSit_Agent_bot` (or Beer's handle `@beerthaish`)

## Step 2 — Daily Developer Diary (SakSee)

**Every day, SakTan reminds SakSee:**

- 📓 **Write 1 diary entry** about today's work
- 🖼️ **Include screenshots** — code, terminal, errors, wins
- 📂 **Save to repo** at `docs/diary/YYYY-MM-DD.md`

**Diary template:**
```markdown
# Diary — 2026-07-04

## What we worked on
- (feature/fix description)

## Hard things
- (bugs, problems, blockers)

## Wins
- (what worked, what shipped)

## Screenshots
- (links to screenshots in assets/diary/)

## Feeling
- (honest — tired, excited, proud, frustrated)
```

**Free tools:**
- Screenshot: built-in OS screenshot tool (free)
- Notes: `docs/diary/` folder in the repo (free)
- Markdown: any text editor (free)

## Step 4 — Weekly Compilation

Every Sunday, SakTan compiles the week into a **public-facing post**:

1. Collect SakSit's 7 stories
2. Collect SakSee's 7 diary entries
3. Write a `Weekly Recap — Week X, July 2026`
4. SakSit posts it as a highlight or carousel
5. Save archive to `docs/diary/weekly/`

This becomes the **permanent record** of how hard the Sak Family works.
