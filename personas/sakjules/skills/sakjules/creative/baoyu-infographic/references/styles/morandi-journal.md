# morandi-journal

Morandi color palette with muted, soft pastel tones. Great for journaling, personal reflection, and mindfulness themes.

## Mood Board
- Primary: #F8F6DA (muted cream) - paper background
- Secondary: #D5C8EB (lavender) - decorative elements
- Accent: #C8A8C3 (sage) - soft highlights
- Text: #6A6B88 (grey)
- Medium: #B8B2DA (midstone)

## Fonts
- Headings: Soft sans-serif (e.g., Josefin Sans Light)
-
Body: Serif, readable, neutral

## Style Traits
- Texture: Soft, flattening gradients
- Illustrations: Minimal, clean graphics, natural elements
- Layout: Symmetrical, with plenty of white space and sectioned blocks
