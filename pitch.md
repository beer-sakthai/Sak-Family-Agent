# Hugging Face Training Job Service

## For AI Engineers & Researchers

You have a model and a dataset. You need to run a training job.
I manage the execution, tracking, and artifact storage for training runs on Hugging Face infrastructure.

---

### ✅ What You Get

- **Managed Training Runs**: Execute training scripts using `accelerate launch`.
- **Reproducibility**: Configuration and code are versioned.
- **Artifact Management**: Final models and logs are stored and versioned.
- **Secure**: Leverages your existing Hugging Face credentials.

---

This service is for orchestrating and managing the lifecycle of model training jobs.
